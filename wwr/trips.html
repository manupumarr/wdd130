Grand Canyon Classic

Experience the majesty of the Grand Canyon from the bottom up. This 7-day excursion takes you through some of the most famous rapids in the world. Includes all meals, camping gear, and professional guides.

  • Duration: 7 Days
  • Difficulty: Advanced

Salmon River Sprint

Perfect for families and beginners. The Salmon River offers beautiful scenery with manageable class II and III rapids. Enjoy fishing breaks and warm sandy beaches for camping at night.

  • Duration: 3 Days
  • Difficulty: Beginner/Intermediate

Desolation Canyon

Don't let the name fool you. This canyon is teeming with wildlife and history. A moderate trip that combines thrilling rapids with hiking opportunities to see ancient petroglyphs.

  • Duration: 5 Days
  • Difficulty: Intermediate
